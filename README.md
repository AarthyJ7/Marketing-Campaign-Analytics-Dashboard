# Marketing Campaign Analytics Dashboard

Interactive marketing analytics dashboard built in Looker Studio using Google Analytics 4 (GA4) ecommerce data. The dashboard enables stakeholders to analyze traffic acquisition, audience engagement, conversion performance, and campaign effectiveness through KPI scorecards, interactive visualizations, and dynamic filtering.

## Business Objective

Marketing teams often struggle to connect traffic acquisition, user engagement, conversion performance, and revenue outcomes into a single view.

This dashboard was designed to answer:

* Which channels drive the most traffic?
* Which channels generate the most revenue?
* How do users engage across devices and audiences?
* Where do users drop off in the purchase journey?
* Which marketing campaigns contribute most to conversions and revenue?

## Tools Used

* Looker Studio
* Google Analytics 4 (GA4)
* Calculated Fields
* Interactive Filters

## Dashboard Story

The dashboard follows the customer journey from acquisition to engagement, conversion, and revenue generation.

### Executive Overview

Provides a high-level view of website performance, traffic, revenue, and conversion metrics.

### Acquisition Performance

Analyzes traffic channels and sources to understand session volume, revenue contribution, and conversion efficiency.

### Audience & Engagement

Explores user behavior across devices, locations, and user types to understand engagement patterns.

### Ecommerce Funnel Analysis

Tracks progression through the purchase journey and highlights key conversion drop-off points.

### Campaign Performance

Evaluates the effectiveness of marketing campaigns in driving traffic, conversions, and revenue.

## Key Insights Delivered

### Executive Insights

* Monitor overall traffic, revenue, and conversion performance.
* Track business trends over time.

### Acquisition Insights

* Identify the channels driving the most sessions and revenue.
* Compare channel conversion performance.

### Audience Insights

* Analyze engagement across devices and locations.
* Compare behavior of new and returning users.

### Funnel Insights

* Identify stages with the highest user drop-off.
* Compare conversion performance across devices.

### Campaign Insights

* Measure campaign contribution to traffic, revenue, and conversions.
